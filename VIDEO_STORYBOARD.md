## **VISUAL EFFECTS LIBRARY**

### **Transitions:**
- **Fade:** Between major sections (0.5s duration)
- **Slide:** For panel appearances (0.3s duration)
- **Wipe:** For page changes (0.4s duration)
- **Zoom:** For focus changes (0.3s duration)

### **Highlights:**
- **Circle highlight:** For specific numbers (red, 2px stroke)
- **Box highlight:** For sections (yellow, subtle shadow)
- **Underline:** For text emphasis (animated draw)
- **Glow:** For active elements (color-matched to brand)

### **Animations:**
- **Counter:** Numbers increment smoothly
- **Progress:** Bars fill left to right
- **Pulse:** Subtle scale 100% to 105% loop
- **Flow:** Particles moving along paths

### **Cursor Effects:**
- **Trail:** Subtle motion blur
- **Click:** Ripple effect (0.4s duration)
- **Hover:** Glow around cursor (subtle)
- **Highlight ring:** Animated ring for emphasis

## **TYPOGRAPHY FOR OVERLAYS**

### **Titles:**
- Font: Inter Bold or Playfair Display
- Size: 48-72px
- Color: Navy #1e3a5f
- Shadow: Subtle drop shadow

### **Body/Labels:**
- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: 18-24px
- Color: Gray #6b7280
- Line height: 1.5

### **Stats/Numbers:**
- Font: Inter Bold
- Size: 36-48px
- Color: Blue #3b82f6
- Animation: Counter effect

### **Callouts:**
- Background: White with 90% opacity
- Border: 2px Gold #d4af37
- Padding: 12px 20px
- Border radius: 8px

---

## **CAMERA MOVEMENTS**

### **Zoom Guidelines:**
- **Maximum zoom:** 125% (avoid pixelation)
- **Minimum zoom:** 100% (native)
- **Speed:** 0.5-0.8s transitions
- **Easing:** Ease-in-out

### **Pan Guidelines:**
- **Speed:** 60-80 pixels/second
- **Smooth:** No sudden stops
- **Anchor:** Keep important elements in frame

### **Scroll Guidelines:**
- **Speed:** 400-500 pixels/second
- **Pause:** 1-2 seconds at each section
- **Smooth:** Ease-in-out timing function

---

## **AUDIO LAYERS**

### **Layer 1: Background Music**
- Style: Corporate/tech ambient
- Volume: 15-20% of narration
- Fade: 2 seconds in/out
- Variation: Subtle changes per act

### **Layer 2: Narration**
- Volume: 100% (reference)
- EQ: Boost clarity (2-4kHz)
- Compression: Moderate
- Noise reduction: Applied

### **Layer 3: UI Sound Effects**
- Click sounds: Subtle, 30% volume
- Notification sounds: 40% volume
- Transition whooshes: 25% volume
- Success chimes: 35% volume

### **Layer 4: Ambient Tech Sounds**
- Data flowing: Very subtle, 10% volume
- Processing sounds: 15% volume
- Only use sparingly for effect

## **RENDERING SPECIFICATIONS**

### **Export Settings:**
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame rate:** 60fps (30fps minimum)
- **Bitrate:** 8-12 Mbps (high quality)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Audio:** AAC, 192kbps, 48kHz

### **File Sizes:**
- 8-minute video at 1080p/60fps: ~480-600MB
- 3-minute version: ~180-225MB
- Consider 4K export for future-proofing
